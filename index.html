Welcome to StudyNet

StudyNet is a learning platform for people wanting to break into the world of networking. Our goal is to provide free access to learning materials to prepare you for certifications such as Network+, Security+, and CCNA.

What Do We Provide

Learning materials for networking fundamentals

We provide lessons that walk users through the first 4 layers of the OSI model. Going from Layer 1 all the way up to Layer 4, we dive into important concepts to give you a solid grasp of how networking works while giving you the tools to study for entry-level certifications.

Highlights

  • Clear, semantic explanations of OSI layers
  • Practice questions and interactive labs
  • Concise diagrams and real-world examples
